Thanks for stopping by!

Hi, I’m Emma. I’m a recent UX/UI design graduate with a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology and a soft spot for pasta.

I grew up in a household that valued practicality over beauty; Beauty was frivolous. Little did I know, my secret shame for the aesthetic world would inspire a career in design. I find myself inspired by eye-catching book covers, the smooth transitions of animated films, the second lives of recycled materials, and the truths revealed in human conversation.

To me, design is a way of reimagining our world.

My Goals

I strive to design products informed by real users. By combining rigorous user research and a creative iterative process, I can help companies succeed in solving real-world problems without sacrificing style.
